### Pro-Tips for Applying Body Sunscreen

- **The Two-Finger Rule**: Apply a generous amount. For the body, aim for about a shot glass-sized amount to cover all exposed areas.
- **Don’t Forget the "Hidden" Spots**: Apply to the tops of your feet, the back of your neck, and your ears.
- **Re-apply Every 2 Hours**: Especially if you are sweating or swimming. Sun sticks are the easiest way to do this without getting your hands messy.
- **Apply 15 Minutes Before Going Out**: Allow the filters to set and form a protective film on your skin.
