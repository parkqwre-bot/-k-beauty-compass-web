### The Evolution of Glass Skin

For years, the K-Beauty world was dominated by "Glass Skin"—that ultra-reflective, dewy, and almost wet look. However, as we enter the summer of 2026, the trend has shifted towards a more sophisticated, "alive" finish. Enter **Cloud Skin** and **Bloom Skin**.

### What is Cloud Skin?

Cloud Skin is the ultimate "soft-matte" finish. Imagine the soft, diffused light of a cloud. It’s not about being flat or dry; it’s about a velvety texture that seems to glow from within. 

**How to achieve it:**
- **Prep with PDRN:** Use a PDRN-infused ampoule to ensure the skin is deeply hydrated and resilient.
- **The Hybrid Base:** Instead of heavy foundations, use a "Skincaring Sunscreen" that provides a slight blurring effect while protecting your skin.
- **Targeted Powdering:** Apply a translucent, hydrating powder only on the T-zone to maintain that soft, ethereal glow on the cheeks.

### What is Bloom Skin?

If Cloud Skin is ethereal, Bloom Skin is vibrant. It mimics the healthy, flushed look of a flower in full bloom—or the skin you have after a perfect night's sleep and a morning jog.

**How to achieve it:**
- **Exosome Therapy:** Start with an exosome serum to boost cellular communication and natural radiance.
- **Cooling Care:** Use a cooling gel cream to lower your skin temperature, which reduces redness and keeps the "bloom" looking fresh rather than overheated.
- **The "Inner Glow" Highlight:** Use a liquid highlighter mixed with your moisturizer for a seamless, natural vitality.

### Summer 2026 Key Ingredients

To maintain these looks under the summer sun, K-Beauty has turned to high-tech bio-regenerative ingredients:
- **PDRN (Salmon DNA):** For intense repair and a firm base.
- **Exosomes:** For "smart" skincare that adapts to your skin's needs.
- **Heartleaf (어성초):** To soothe the skin from heat and environmental stress.

### Conclusion

Whether you prefer the soft-focus elegance of **Cloud Skin** or the healthy vitality of **Bloom Skin**, Summer 2026 is all about skin that looks healthy, cared for, and technologically advanced. Stay protected with a high-quality Korean sunscreen and enjoy the glow!
