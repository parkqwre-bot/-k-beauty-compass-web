### What are Hydrogel Masks?

Unlike traditional sheet masks made of cotton or microfiber, hydrogel masks are made from a **solidified essence** that feels like a thin, jelly-like sheet. These masks are typically 100% water-soluble and are designed to react to your skin's temperature.

### Why Hydrogel is Superior for Summer Cooling

1. **Lower Surface Temperature:** Hydrogel has a naturally high heat capacity. Even at room temperature, it feels significantly cooler than a cotton sheet. When applied, it can drop the skin's surface temperature by several degrees almost instantly.
2. **Superior Adhesion:** Because they have a "second-skin" texture, hydrogel masks cling perfectly to the contours of your face. This prevents the "evaporative cooling" that can sometimes dry out a regular sheet mask too quickly in a hot, dry environment.
3. **Timed Release of Active Ingredients:** As the mask warms up against your skin, it slowly "melts," releasing the concentrated essence directly into your pores without dripping.

### Trending Hydrogel Ingredients for June 2026

This summer, look for these specific ingredient combinations in your hydrogel masks:

*   **Guaiazulene + Panthenol:** For those with extreme sun sensitivity, this combination calms the "burning" sensation and repairs the skin barrier.
*   **Glacier Water + Hyaluronic Acid:** Provides a "deep freeze" effect that is perfect for reducing morning puffiness or evening heat exhaustion.
*   **Fermented Black Tea (Kombucha):** A 2026 favorite for detoxifying skin that has been exposed to summer humidity and pollution.

### Pro-Tip: The "Fridge-to-Face" Method

For the ultimate 2026 summer spa experience, store your hydrogel masks in a dedicated skincare fridge (or your regular refrigerator). Apply the chilled mask while lying down for 20 minutes. The hydrogel will lock in the cold much longer than a standard mask, providing a sustained cooling effect that reduces inflammation and tightens pores.
