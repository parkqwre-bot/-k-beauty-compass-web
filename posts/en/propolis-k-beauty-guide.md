## What is Propolis?

If you've ever wondered how K-Beauty stars achieve that "honey skin" look, the answer often lies in one powerhouse ingredient: **Propolis**. 

Propolis is a resin-like substance made by bees from the buds of cone-bearing trees. Bees use it to build their hives, but in skincare, it acts as a miraculous healing and protective agent. It is naturally antibacterial, anti-inflammatory, and rich in antioxidants.

## Why Propolis is a Must-Have in 2026

As we move through 2026, the K-Beauty trend is shifting towards "Skin Resilience"—strengthening the barrier rather than just exfoliating it. Propolis fits perfectly into this philosophy.

### Key Benefits:
1.  **Acne Fighting:** Its antibacterial properties make it a gentle but effective treatment for acne-prone skin.
2.  **The "Glow" Factor:** It instantly hydrates and gives the skin a dewy, plump finish known as *chok-chok*.
3.  **Soothing Redness:** Perfect for sensitive skin or rosacea, it calms inflammation quickly.
4.  **Anti-Aging:** Packed with antioxidants, it fights free radicals that cause premature aging.
