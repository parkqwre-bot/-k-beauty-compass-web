### What are Copper Peptides?

Copper Peptides (specifically **Copper Tripeptide-1** or **GHK-Cu**) are naturally occurring complexes that combine the element copper with three amino acids. In the human body, they play a crucial role in wound healing and anti-inflammatory processes. 

In skincare, they act as "signaling molecules," telling your skin cells to produce more collagen and elastin—the building blocks of firm, youthful skin.

### How to Use Copper Peptides in Your K-Beauty Routine

To get the most out of your "Blue Droplets," follow these tips:

1.  **The "Actives" Rule:** Do not mix Copper Peptides in the same routine as high-percentage Vitamin C (L-Ascorbic Acid) or strong AHAs/BHAs. These acidic ingredients can break the bond between the copper and the peptides, rendering them ineffective.
2.  **Order of Application:** Apply your Copper Peptide serum after your toner and essence, but before heavier creams or oils.
3.  **Nighttime Synergy:** Pair them with a Ceramide-rich moisturizer at night to lock in the repair signals while you sleep.
