### What Exactly is a Sun Stick?

A sun stick is a solid, balm-like sunscreen that comes in a twist-up tube, similar to a deodorant stick. This innovative format offers several advantages over traditional liquid sunscreens:

*   **Convenience:** The compact, portable design makes it perfect for on-the-go application. Just toss it in your bag and you're ready for touch-ups anytime, anywhere.
*   **Hygienic Application:** You can apply it directly to your face and body without using your hands, minimizing the transfer of bacteria and germs.
*   **Easy Reapplication:** Reapplying sunscreen every two hours is crucial for proper sun protection. Sun sticks make this process incredibly simple and mess-free, even over makeup.

### Key Ingredients to Look For in 2025

Korean sun sticks are not just about sun protection; they are often packed with beneficial skincare ingredients. Here are some key ingredients to look for:

*   **Centella Asiatica (Cica):** Known for its soothing and healing properties, Cica is perfect for calming irritated or sensitive skin.
*   **Hyaluronic Acid:** This powerhouse hydrator attracts moisture to the skin, keeping it plump and hydrated throughout the day.
*   **Niacinamide:** A multi-tasking ingredient that helps to brighten the skin, improve texture, and strengthen the skin barrier.
*   **Birch Juice:** Rich in minerals and amino acids, birch juice provides intense hydration and nourishment.

### How to Use a Sun Stick Correctly

To get the most out of your sun stick, follow these simple steps:

1.  Twist up the product by about 1cm.
2.  Glide the stick generously over all areas exposed to the sun.
3.  For even coverage, go over each area 2-3 times.
4.  When applying to the face, be sure to cover often-missed spots like the ears, neck, and around the eyes.
5.  Reapply at least every two hours, or more frequently if you are swimming or sweating.
