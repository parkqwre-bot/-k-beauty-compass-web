### What Exactly is a K-Beauty Ampoule?

Think of an ampoule as a super-concentrated serum with a higher percentage of active ingredients. While serums are designed for daily use to tackle long-term skin concerns, ampoules are formulated for targeted, short-term treatments. They often come in smaller bottles or single-use vials because they are meant to be used for a finite period to address a specific issue, like calming a breakout, intensely hydrating dry patches, or boosting radiance before a big event.

### The Ampoule Advantage: Key Benefits

- **High Potency:** Ampoules deliver a powerful dose of ingredients like Vitamin C, hyaluronic acid, or peptides directly to your skin.
- **Targeted Solutions:** Whether you're dealing with dullness, dehydration, or loss of firmness, there's an ampoule designed to fix it.
- **Fast Results:** Due to their high concentration, you can often see visible improvements in your skin in a shorter amount of time compared to standard serums.

### How to Incorporate an Ampoule into Your Routine

Ampoules are most effective when applied to clean skin after toning.

1.  **Cleanse** and **Tone** your face.
2.  Apply a few drops of the **ampoule** directly to your skin, gently patting it in for maximum absorption.
3.  Follow up with your regular **serum** (optional, depending on your needs), **moisturizer**, and **sunscreen** in the morning.

Use it for a few weeks as a "booster treatment" whenever your skin needs extra help.
