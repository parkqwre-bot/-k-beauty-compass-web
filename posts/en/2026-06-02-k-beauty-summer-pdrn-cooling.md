### 1. The Power of PDRN (Skin Regeneration)

PDRN (Polydeoxyribonucleotide), often derived from salmon DNA, has become the "it" ingredient of 2026. Known for its incredible ability to accelerate tissue repair and boost collagen production, it’s being hailed as the next-generation alternative to Retinol—without the irritation.

**Why it's essential for Summer:**
Sun exposure can damage the skin barrier and lead to "heat aging." PDRN works deep within the dermis to repair this damage, ensuring your skin remains firm and resilient even under the harsh sun.

### 2. Cooling Care & "After Feel"

With temperatures soaring, "Cooling Care" is no longer optional. But in 2026, it's not just about the immediate cooling sensation; it's about the **"After Feel"**—how the skin feels hours after application. Consumers are demanding products that lower skin temperature without leaving a sticky residue.

**How to Lower Your Skin Temperature:**
High skin temperature causes sebum overproduction and pore enlargement. Using products with ingredients like Menthyl Lactate or specialized cooling polymers can reduce skin heat by up to 5°C instantly.

### 3. Achieving "Bloom Skin"

"Bloom Skin" is all about that natural, petal-soft glow that looks like it's coming from within. It’s achieved through meticulous **Hydration Layering**.

**The Routine:**
1. **Cool Down:** Start with a cooling toner pad to reset your skin temperature.
2. **Regenerate:** Apply a PDRN serum to provide the nutrients needed for cell repair.
3. **Protect:** Use a "Sun Serum" that offers high SPF protection with a weightless, non-sticky after-feel.
