### What is Mugwort (Artemisia) in K-Beauty?

Mugwort, or **Artemisia**, has been used in Korean traditional medicine for centuries. In the world of K-Beauty, it has emerged as a powerhouse ingredient, often compared to Cica (Centella Asiatica) for its incredible skin-soothing properties. Specifically, **Artemisia Princeps** (Korean Mugwort) is harvested from regions like Ganghwa Island, known for its clean environment and potent herbal quality.

### Top Benefits of Mugwort for Your Skin

1. **Anti-Inflammatory**: Mugwort is naturally anti-inflammatory, making it perfect for reducing redness, rosacea, and acne-related irritation.
2. **Antibacterial & Antifungal**: It helps clear up skin impurities and prevents breakouts by keeping harmful bacteria at bay.
3. **Deep Hydration**: Unlike some calming ingredients that can be drying, Mugwort provides a boost of moisture while calming the skin.
4. **Rich in Antioxidants**: Packed with Vitamin A and C, it protects the skin from environmental stressors and brightens the complexion.
