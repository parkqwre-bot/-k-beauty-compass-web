For a long time, K-Beauty was synonymous with a meticulous 10-step facial routine. But in 2026, the focus has expanded beyond the face. The "Body Glass Skin" trend is taking the world by storm, as more people realize that the skin on their body deserves as much care and attention as their face.

As summer approaches and we start to wear shorter sleeves and shorts, now is the perfect time to upgrade your body care routine. Here is the ultimate K-Beauty guide to achieving radiant, smooth skin from head to toe.

### 1. Gentle Exfoliation: The Foundation of Glow
Just like your face, dead skin cells can accumulate on your body, leading to a dull appearance and rough texture. K-Beauty body care emphasizes gentle, chemical exfoliation over harsh physical scrubs.

Look for body washes or pads containing:
*   **AHA (Alpha Hydroxy Acid):** Great for smoothing surface texture.
*   **BHA (Beta Hydroxy Acid):** Ideal for clearing out pores and preventing body acne.
*   **PHA (Poly Hydroxy Acid):** A gentle alternative for sensitive skin.

Using an exfoliating mitt (Italy Towel) once a week is still a beloved Korean tradition, but modern K-Beauty products offer a more chemical-focused, daily approach.

### 2. Targeting "Backne" and "Chestne"
With the increase in outdoor activities and sweating during the warmer months, body acne (backne and chestne) can become a concern. K-Beauty treats body acne with the same calming ingredients used for the face.

Ingredients like **Heartleaf**, **Cica (Centella Asiatica)**, and **Tea Tree** are now found in convenient body sprays and targeted treatments. These ingredients help soothe inflammation and balance oil production without drying out the skin.

### 3. Deep Hydration: The Secret to the "Glass" Effect
The key to that "Glass Skin" look on your body is intense, layered hydration. It’s not just about applying a heavy cream once a day; it’s about ensuring your skin stays plump and hydrated throughout the day.

*   **Body Lotions with Ceramides:** Help strengthen the skin barrier and lock in moisture.
*   **Hyaluronic Acid Serums for the Body:** Lightweight formulas that sink in quickly and provide an instant hydration boost.
*   **Body Oils:** For an extra glow, apply a thin layer of body oil over your moisturizer to seal everything in.

### 4. Don't Forget Body Sun Protection
Sunscreen isn't just for your face! If you want to prevent premature aging and dark spots on your body, daily sun protection is a must. 

K-Beauty has revolutionized body sun care with:
*   **Sun Sticks:** Mess-free and perfect for reapplication on arms and legs.
*   **Cooling Sun Gels:** Lightweight formulas that lower skin temperature on contact, perfect for hot summer days.

### Conclusion
Achieving "Body Glass Skin" is all about consistency and using the right ingredients. By treating your body skin with the same respect you give your face, you’ll be ready to glow all summer long.
