### Get Ready for Summer: Pore and Sebum Control the K-Beauty Way

As we enter May, the rise in temperature and humidity directly affects our skin. One of the most common concerns during this transition is increased sebum production and more visible pores. In 2026, K-Beauty continues to lead the way with innovative, gentle, yet effective solutions for maintaining a clear and matte complexion.

### Why Your Pores Need Extra Love in May

When the weather gets warmer, your skin produces more oil to keep itself hydrated. However, when this excess oil mixes with dead skin cells and environmental pollutants, it can clog your pores, leading to blackheads, breakouts, and a textured appearance. Proper management now is the key to achieving that smooth "glass skin" look all summer long.

### 1. Deep Cleansing with Soothing Ingredients

The foundation of pore care is thorough cleansing. Look for oil cleansers that emulsify quickly and contain soothing ingredients like Heartleaf (Houttuynia Cordata) or Mugwort. These ingredients help to dissolve sebum while calming any inflammation.

Popular choices like the **Anua Heartleaf Pore Control Cleansing Oil** or **Manyo Factory Pure Cleansing Oil** are excellent for melting away stubborn blackheads without stripping the skin's natural moisture barrier.

### 2. Gentle Exfoliation: PHA and LHA

Forget harsh physical scrubs. In 2026, K-Beauty favors chemical exfoliants like PHA (Polyhydroxy Acids) and LHA (Lipohydroxy Acids). These are much gentler than AHA or BHA, making them suitable for daily use even on sensitive skin.

Using a toner pad soaked in PHA, such as the **Isntree Chestnut PHA 5% Clear Toner**, helps to refine skin texture and keep pores clear of debris. LHA is particularly effective for those with oily skin as it is oil-soluble and penetrates deep into the pores.

### 3. Pore-Tightening Serums with Niacinamide

Niacinamide remains a superstar ingredient for a reason. It helps regulate oil production and visibly improves the appearance of enlarged pores. Look for serums that combine Niacinamide with natural tannins or zinc for an added astringent effect.

Products containing **Galactomyces Ferment Filtrate** also work wonders for balancing sebum while brightening the overall skin tone, giving you that healthy K-Beauty glow.

### 4. Weekly Detox with Volcanic Clay Masks

For a deeper detox, a weekly clay mask is essential. Korean volcanic ash, particularly from Jeju Island, is famous for its powerful absorption properties.

The **Innisfree Super Volcanic Pore Clay Mask 2X** is a classic that continues to be a favorite in 2026. It effectively draws out impurities and minimizes the look of pores in just 10 minutes.

### Conclusion: Consistency is Key

Managing pores and sebum is not a one-time task but a consistent habit. By incorporating these K-Beauty essentials into your routine this May, you can ensure your skin stays fresh, clear, and ready for the summer heat.
