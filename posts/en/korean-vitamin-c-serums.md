## Why Korean Vitamin C Serums Are Superior

Vitamin C is a powerhouse ingredient known for its ability to brighten skin, fade dark spots (hyperpigmentation), and stimulate collagen production. Korean skincare brands have mastered the art of formulating Vitamin C serums that are effective yet gentle, often combining pure ascorbic acid with soothing ingredients to minimize irritation.

In 2026, the trend in K-Beauty is toward stable, high-performance Vitamin C derivatives and innovative delivery systems that ensure maximum absorption without the stinging sensation often associated with Western counterparts.

## Benefits of Vitamin C for Your Skin

*   **Brightening:** Fades acne scars, sun spots, and uneven skin tone.
*   **Anti-Aging:** Boosts collagen production to reduce fine lines and wrinkles.
*   **Protection:** Acts as a potent antioxidant, protecting skin from free radical damage caused by UV rays and pollution.
*   **Glow:** Restores radiance to dull, tired-looking skin.

## How to Use Vitamin C in Your Routine

1.  **Cleanse:** Start with a gentle cleanser.
2.  **Tone:** Apply a hydrating toner.
3.  **Treat:** Apply a few drops of Vitamin C serum. *Tip: If you are using pure Ascorbic Acid, you may feel a slight tingling sensation.*
4.  **Moisturize:** Lock it in with a moisturizer.
5.  **SPF (Morning):** **Crucial!** Vitamin C makes your skin more sensitive to the sun, but it also boosts the efficacy of your sunscreen. Always wear SPF 50+ during the day.

## Storage Tips
*   Store pure Vitamin C serums in the **refrigerator** to prevent oxidation (turning brown).
*   Stable derivatives (like Goodal) can usually be kept in a cool, dark place.
