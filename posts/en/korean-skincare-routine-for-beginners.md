The Korean skincare routine is famous worldwide for its focus on achieving healthy, glowing skin. While the 10-step routine might sound intimidating, it's a customizable framework you can adapt to your skin's needs. This guide will break down the core steps for beginners.

### The Core Philosophy

Korean skincare is not just about products; it's about a gentle, consistent approach. The goal is to nourish and protect your skin, not strip it with harsh chemicals. Hydration is key!

### The (Simplified) 10-Step Routine for Beginners

**Morning Routine (Focus on Protection):**
1.  **Cleanser:** Use a gentle water-based cleanser.
2.  **Toner:** Hydrate and balance your skin's pH.
3.  **Moisturizer:** Lock in moisture.
4.  **Sunscreen:** The most crucial step! Protect your skin from UV damage.

**Evening Routine (Focus on Repair and Treatment):**
1.  **Oil-Based Cleanser:** Melt away makeup, sunscreen, and impurities.
2.  **Water-Based Cleanser:** (Double Cleanse!) Wash away any remaining residue.
3.  **Exfoliator (1-2 times a week):** Gently slough off dead skin cells.
4.  **Toner:** Prep your skin for the next steps.
5.  **Serum/Ampoule:** Target specific concerns like acne, fine lines, or hyperpigmentation.
6.  **Moisturizer:** A richer cream for nighttime repair.
