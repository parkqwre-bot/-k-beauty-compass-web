Reapplying sunscreen every two hours is the golden rule of skincare, but let’s be honest: it’s a nightmare when you’re wearing a full face of makeup. Rubbing a cream destroys your foundation, and sun sticks can sometimes drag or move your base products.

Enter the **Korean Sun Cushion**—the game-changing innovation that makes SPF reapplication easy, hygienic, and even beautifying.

In 2026, Sun Cushions have evolved beyond just "sunscreen in a compact." They now offer cooling benefits, tone-up effects, and skincare ingredients that refresh your look while protecting your skin.

### What is a Sun Cushion?

A Sun Cushion looks exactly like a cushion foundation (compact case + mirror + puff), but the sponge inside is soaked in a lightweight, liquid sunscreen instead of makeup.

**Why they are superior for reapplication:**
*   **Tap, Don't Rub:** You apply it by patting the puff on your skin, which presses the SPF *over* your makeup without smearing it.
*   **Cooling Effect:** Most formulas lower skin temperature immediately upon contact, which is crucial for preventing "thermal aging" (heat-induced inflammation).
*   **Tone-Up:** Many mineral-based sun cushions offer a slight brightening effect, fixing oxidation or dullness that happens to makeup throughout the day.
*   **Hands-Free:** No sticky hands. Perfect for on-the-go.

### How to Use a Sun Cushion Correctly

1.  **Press:** Press the puff into the sponge to pick up product.
2.  **Pat:** Gently pat (do not drag!) the puff all over your face.
3.  **Layer:** Apply a second layer to high points of the face (cheeks, nose, forehead) where the sun hits hardest.
4.  **Clean:** Wash your puff weekly or replace it to prevent bacterial breakouts.
