Spring in Korea brings beautiful cherry blossoms but it also brings the challenge of fine dust and yellow dust. These environmental factors can clog pores lead to inflammation and cause skin sensitivity. Here is how you can protect your skin using K-Beauty wisdom.

### 1. The Power of Double Cleansing
The most important step is removing microscopic dust particles. Start with an oil-based cleanser to dissolve impurities followed by a gentle water-based foam cleanser. This ensures your pores are truly clean without stripping the moisture barrier.

### 2. Barrier Repair with Ceramides and Panthenol
Fine dust can weaken your skin barrier. Look for moisturizers containing Ceramides or Panthenol. These ingredients act like a shield strengthening your skin surface against external irritants.

### 3. Soothing with Mugwort and Heartleaf
If your skin feels itchy or red due to pollen incorporate soothing ingredients. Korean Mugwort (Ssuk) and Heartleaf (Eoseongcho) are famous for their anti-inflammatory properties and are perfect for calming spring-time sensitivity.

### 4. Physical Sunscreen as a Shield
Non-nano physical sunscreens containing Zinc Oxide or Titanium Dioxide provide a physical layer of protection that can help prevent dust particles from directly touching your skin.

By following these steps you can enjoy the spring weather while keeping your complexion clear and healthy.
